Broccoli Salad

Description

This vibrant broccoli salad features fresh broccoli florets, crunchy red onion slices, crispy bacon bits, and a sprinkle of nuts or seeds.

The ingredients are all mixed together and coated with a creamy dressing, making it a delicious and visually appealing dish.

Perfect for a healthy and flavorful meal!

Ingredients

  • Broccoli florets
  • Red onion
  • Bacon
  • Dried Cranberries
  • Sunflower seeds
  • Cheddar Cheese
  • Mayonnaise
  • Olive Oil
  • Sugar
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Chop the broccoli: Cut the broccoli into small florets and place them in a large bowl.
  2. Prepare the other ingredients: Dice the red onion, crumble the bacon, and shred the cheddar cheese.
  3. Add these to the bowl with the broccoli.
  4. Mix in the dried cranberries and sunflower seeds.
  5. Make the dressing: In a separate bowl, whisk together the mayonnaise, olive oil, and sugar until smooth.
  6. Combine everything: Pour the dressing over the broccoli mixture and toss until everything is well coated.
  7. Season: Add salt and pepper to taste.
  8. Chill: Cover the salad and refrigerate for at least an hour before serving to allow the flavors to meld.
